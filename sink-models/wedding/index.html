Luxury Portable Sink with Vessel Basin Model PSE-010

Elegant but sturdy, the luxury portable sink with vessel basin offers a delicate and fashionable look ideal for events that require a touch of style. The vessel-like sink on top offers a nice ornamental accent which is particularly popular for weddings and other stylish events. Although it is dainty, this luxury portable sink offers a large 6-gallon tank with ample pressure with both cold to hot water. Additionally, this model is compact and lightweight making it easy to accommodate indoor and outdoor events. To top off the look, the model comes in black, white, grey, red, blue, or maple. If your event requires that every little detail is perfect, the luxury portable sink is the ideal match for you.

Specifications:
  • Compact and lightweight and suitable for indoor and outdoor usage
  • Contains one 6 gallon tank for fresh water, and one 7 gallons tank for waste water
  • Includes an Adjustable water heater that can be set from 95 degrees to 155 degrees Fahrenheit
  • Comes with four swivel casters with locking brakes
  • Available in 6 colors: Black, White, Grey, Red, Blue, or Maple
